About Me

Who Am I?

Hi, I'm Julia Huynh.

I'm a recent User Experience Design graduate from Wilfrid Laurier. I am particularly passionate in graphic design. I come from an art background and I love designing and coming up with new ideas. Ever since I was young, I have loved to tinker.

Over the years, I gained a well rounded background graphic design through various workplace exposure. From my co-op as a User Experience Design Intern at BlueCat Networks, I had a project where I created a variety of graphics for the Learning team's website. From my job at Laurier International, I created a variety of promotional event posters. In my most recent job, at Digital Main Street, I worked as an eCommerce Advisor. Part of my role included working on clients' websites to improve their User Interface.
