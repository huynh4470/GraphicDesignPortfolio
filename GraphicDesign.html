Graphic Design

I am passionate in graphic design and have gained experience in a workplace setting. Here are the graphic design projects, I have worked on:

Promotional Event Posters for Laurier International

While working at Laurier International, I was in charge of making the event posters. I ensured that all the event details are visible and displayed in a stylist manner that matches the theme of the event.


BPL Procedural Document

I did a project with the Brantford Public Library on their self checkout machine. My task was to create a document that outlines how to use the machine. For this project, I created graphics on Adobe Illustrator, then wrote the content and put everything together in InDesign.


Book Cover

I did some freelance work for a writter, who wrote a poetry book about her life experience. I produced some bookcovers for her in photoshop.
